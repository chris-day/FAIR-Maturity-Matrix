
[FAIR Personas](FAIRMM_personas.md) can play different roles with respect to FAIR data. They typically produce, consume, or enable FAIR data.  While each person may have a have a primary role, these are not mutually exclusive.  While several organization in the FAIR community of experts use this terminology in internal communications, we could not find obvious publicly available definitions.

## Note on disambiguation
The FAIRMM version 1.2 dimension 4 is called "FAIR personas". In FAIRMM Versions 1.0 and 1.1 the same dimension is called "FAIR roles"
