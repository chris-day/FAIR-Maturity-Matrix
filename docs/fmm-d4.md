#  **FAIR personas**

We address the personas required in an organization to implement FAIR principles in this dimension. What are the jobs to do? Who would ensure that happens? Who are the people responsible for FAIR implementation? These personas will ensure, among others, project execution to build capabilities and operational roles maintaining FAIR processes, infrastructure, governance, as well as data and metadata. The personas can play different roles, for example they can be primarily data consumers or data producers. 
The personas carry and deploy FAIR knowledge, and can benefit from different types [FAIR training](FAIR_training.md). A higher maturity levels some of the organization knowledge is also coded in the Tools and infrastructure as well as the data itself.
