## Data_Producer 

## Role definition of Data_Producer
A person, team, or system that creates or collects data and is responsible for ensuring the data (and its accompanying metadata) is prepared for FAIR reuse (findable, accessible under defined conditions, interoperable, reusable). The role is closely tied to data stewardship practices across the data lifecycle.
